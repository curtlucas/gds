About Green Diamond

Green Diamond is a professional services company specializing in Sustainability Intelligence, the next generation of enterprise business intelligence and corporate performance management. Through the use of strategy consulting and technology implementations, our comprehensive solutions help companies and government agencies manage, maximize, and sustain optimal organizational performance. Green Diamond enables organizations to more powerfully measure, analyze, and understand the full spectrum of performance as it relates to the triple bottom line (3BL): Profit + People + Planet.

Our mission

Provide best-in-class business intelligence solutions for sustainable performance.

Our vision

Accelerate enterprise performance and enhance profitability through smart sustainability efforts.

Finding the right solution for you at all levels
